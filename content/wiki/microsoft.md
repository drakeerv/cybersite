# Microsoft Corporation

The **Microsoft Corporation** is an American multinational technology company with headquarters in Redmond, Washington. It is widely known for developing, manufacturing, licensing, supporting, and selling computer software, consumer electronics, personal computers, and related services [1].

Founded by Bill Gates and Paul Allen on April 4, 1975, Microsoft rose to dominate the personal computer operating system market with MS-DOS and later, Microsoft Windows. The company's strategy of strong backwards compatibility and licensing agreements helped cement its position in the 1980s and 1990s.

## Modern Business Segments

Microsoft currently operates three main business segments:

1.  **Productivity and Business Processes:** Includes Office 365, LinkedIn, and Dynamics 365.
2.  **Intelligent Cloud:** Dominated by Azure, one of the world's leading cloud computing platforms.
3.  **More Personal Computing:** Encompasses Windows, Surface devices, and Xbox.
